## 🔍 About Me

I am a **Results‑driven Data & Systems Specialist** with over 14 years of experience across finance, auditing, operations, and megaproject reporting. My passion lies in transforming complex, multi-system engineering and operational data into clear, actionable business intelligence that drives strategic decisions for €4.5B+ global projects.

Working across diverse, large-scale environments—from green battery manufacturing to green steel megaprojects—has taught me that the best insights come from understanding both the data *and* the people behind it. I don't just build ETL pipelines; I bridge the gap between raw system inputs and high-level strategy, always with an eye on efficiency, compliance, and human impact.

* 📍 **Location:** Luleå, Sweden 🌍
* 🔭 **Current Focus:** Leading end-to-end reporting for one of Europe's most advanced green steel megaprojects at IDOM.
* ⚡ **Core Strengths:** Microsoft Fabric, Power BI, SQL, Python, Azure, and turning chaos into clarity.
* 💬 **On a personal note:** I believe in hiring for attitude, leading with empathy, and creating opportunities for English-speaking talent in Sweden. Let's build a more inclusive workforce.
